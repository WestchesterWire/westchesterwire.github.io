🚨 Breaking

Restaurant Week starts Monday across the Hudson Valley — and Westchester is all over it

By the Wire Staff  ·  Sunday, Mar. 1

Hudson Valley Restaurant Week Spring 2026 begins Monday, March 2 and runs through Sunday, March 15, with participating restaurants across Westchester County and the wider region.

County officials promoted this spring cycle this week, including a kickoff at the Zwilling Cooking Studio. For local diners, it means two weeks of prix-fixe menus and tight reservation windows.

If you want prime dinner slots, book early. March calendars fill quickly once Restaurant Week starts, especially in popular corridors like White Plains, Bronxville, and Southern Westchester.

"Hudson Valley Restaurant Week Spring 2026 kicks off March 2 and runs through March 15."

This two-week run is also a late-winter boost for hospitality workers and local business owners. If you have a favorite neighborhood spot, this is a good week to show up for them.

🏛 Politics & Government

County increases patrols near houses of worship through spring observances

Westchester County Police announced increased patrols during current and upcoming religious observances, covering a period that includes Ramadan, Passover, Lent, and Easter.

Officials described the deployment as a proactive safety measure. For congregations and families, expect a more visible law-enforcement presence at high-attendance services and events in the coming weeks.
